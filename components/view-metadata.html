What is EXIF Data?

  • Digital Footprint: EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) is hidden data stored inside your photos. It tells the story of how, when, and where a picture was taken.
  • Photographer's Best Friend: See the exact settings (ISO, Aperture, Shutter Speed) used by professionals to capture stunning shots, allowing you to learn from their settings.
  • Location Tracking: If Location Services were on, your smartphone embeds exact GPS coordinates. Our tool plots this directly onto a Live Google Map!
  • 100% Private Tool: Our EXIF Viewer runs entirely locally in your browser. We do not upload your sensitive photos or location data to any servers.
