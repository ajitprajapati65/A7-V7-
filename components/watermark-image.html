Why Watermark Your Images?

  • Copyright Protection: Prevent unauthorized use and theft of your original photos and artworks.
  • Brand Awareness: Add your company logo or website URL so people know who created the content when it gets shared.
  • Repeating Tile Pattern: The best way to protect images. Our "Repeat" feature covers the entire photo, making it impossible for someone to simply crop the watermark out.
  • 100% Private: Your photos are processed entirely within your web browser. No uploads to servers mean your original, un-watermarked files stay completely safe.
