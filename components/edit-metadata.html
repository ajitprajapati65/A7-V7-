Why Edit or Remove EXIF Metadata?

  • Privacy Protection: When you take a photo with your smartphone, it silently saves your exact GPS location, time, and phone model inside the file. Use our "Privacy Shield" to wipe this data before sharing photos online.
  • Copyrighting: Professional photographers can inject their Artist Name and Copyright details directly into the image file so they get credit wherever the image goes.
  • Fixing Details: Did your camera have the wrong date setting? Easily correct the 'Date Taken' field so your digital albums organize properly.
  • 100% Safe: Reading and editing metadata is done locally on your browser. Your private photos are never uploaded to any server.
