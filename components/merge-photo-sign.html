Why Use Our Merge Tool?

  • Magic Signature Blend: Clicked a picture of your signature on a slightly grey or yellowish paper? Our "Magic Blend" automatically removes the paper background, keeping only your ink!
  • Independent Controls: Zoom and move your passport photo and your signature independently. Just click on the respective section and drag it to fit perfectly.
  • Official Standards: Outputs a perfectly proportioned image (usually required as 400x550 pixels) with optional separator lines and borders accepted by major exam portals like SSC and UPSC.
  • 100% Secure: Forms require sensitive documents. This tool works locally in your browser. Your photo and signature are NEVER sent to any server.
