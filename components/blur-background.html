How It Works

  1. Upload Your Image - Drag & drop or browse to select the photo you want to edit.
  2. Smart AI Scanning - Our optimized AI instantly scans the image to separate the main subject from the background with high accuracy.
  3. Adjust Background Blur - Use the real-time slider to control how soft or strong you want the background blur to be (Level 0-25).
  4. Refine with Edge Smoothness - Fine-tune the edges around your subject using the new smoothness slider for a flawless, natural blend.
  5. Download HQ Output - Once you are perfectly satisfied with the result, hit download to save your high-quality portrait image.

That's it - a perfect studio-style photo ready in seconds!
