What is Freehand Cropping?

  • Ultimate Control: Unlike square or circle crops, the Freehand (Lasso) tool lets you trace the exact outline of a person, car, pet, or product.
  • Transparent Backgrounds: Everything outside your drawn line becomes transparent. The image downloads as a high-quality PNG to preserve this transparency.
  • Auto-Trim Magic: Our smart algorithm detects the exact size of your cut-out object and automatically shrinks the canvas to fit it perfectly, removing useless empty space.
  • 100% Secure & Fast: All the heavy graphic processing is done via HTML5 Canvas directly in your web browser. Zero server uploads!
