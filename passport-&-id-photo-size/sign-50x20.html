Why 50mm x 20mm Signature?

  • Official Standards: Portals like MPSC (Maharashtra PSC) strictly require the signature to be exactly 5.0 cm wide and 2.0 cm high.
  • Aspect Ratio Locked: This tool locks your crop box to a 2.5 ratio (50/20). You just need to fit your signature inside the box; we handle the math.
  • Automatic White Background: Government portals reject transparent PNGs. Our system automatically places your cropped signature on a solid white background.
  • File Size Control: Signatures usually need to be under 20KB or 50KB. Use the quality slider to shrink the file size before downloading.
