About 4x6 Inch Jumbo Photos

  • The Standard Postcard: The 4x6 inch format is the most popular size in the world for physical photo printing, framing, and family albums.
  • Portrait vs Landscape: Our tool supports both formats. "Portrait" is tall (4 inches wide, 6 inches tall) ideal for single-person photos. "Landscape" is wide (6 inches wide, 4 inches tall), perfect for group photos or scenic shots.
  • Studio Grade DPI: Photo labs require exactly 300 DPI for a crisp print without blurriness. We automatically generate a massive 1200 x 1800 pixels file to match professional studio standards.
  • Border Magic: If you upload a PNG image with a transparent background, you can use the "Background Fill" option to instantly add a clean white or black border to your final print.
