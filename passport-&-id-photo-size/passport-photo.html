How to Make the Perfect ID Photo

  • Look Straight: Ensure you are looking directly at the camera with both ears visible.
  • Align the Face: Use your mouse to drag and zoom the image inside the crop box. Your face should take up about 70-80% of the box.
  • Auto-Resizing: You don't need to worry about pixels or dimensions. Select your document type, and we automatically generate the exact pixel size required at 300 DPI (Standard for printing).
  • Solid Backgrounds: Official documents require solid backgrounds. If you used our "Remove Background" tool first, upload that transparent PNG here, and choose White or Blue as your background color.
