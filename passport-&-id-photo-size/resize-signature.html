Perfecting Your Signature Photo

  • Clear Paper: Always use clear white, unruled paper for your signature. Grain or lines on the paper may cause the portal to reject the image.
  • Dark Pen: Use a dark black or blue ink pen (preferably gel or ballpoint). Do not use pencil.
  • Automatic Brightening: To help portals process your signature, we recommend using our "Remove Background" tool first to get a transparent background, then uploading it here to place it on a clean white background.
  • DPI Settings: Most portals require signatures at 200 or 300 DPI. We calculate dimensions at 200 DPI, which is standard. For exact centimeters/inches, please convert based on your required DPI.
  • Handling Padding: Banks often reject signature images that are cropped too tightly. Use our "Signature Padding" slider to add a smooth white buffer around the edges.
