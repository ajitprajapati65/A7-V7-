Digital 600x600 Upload Guidelines

  • Perfect Square: 600x600 pixels means a 1:1 aspect ratio. Our tool strictly locks your crop box into a perfect square so it can't be stretched or squashed.
  • Head Sizing: Just like a 2x2 inch physical photo, your head should take up about 50% to 69% of the height. Keep your face perfectly in the center.
  • No Scanning Needed: If you have a normal selfie, simply upload it here. Downloading directly from this tool provides a native digital file, which is much cleaner than scanning a physical photo.
  • File Size (KB): The US State Department and most online applications require the file to be under 240 KB. The default 85% quality slider will easily keep your photo under this limit while preserving high clarity.
