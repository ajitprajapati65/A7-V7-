Why Compress Images?

  • Form Uploads: Government portals, job applications, and university forms strictly require photos under 50KB or 100KB. This tool helps you hit that target instantly.
  • Website Speed: If you are a web developer or blogger, uploading 5MB images will slow down your site. Compressing them to WebP/JPG speeds up loading times significantly.
  • Save Storage Space: Shrink large camera photos to free up space on your phone or Google Drive without making the photos look blurry.

How Our Pro Compressor Works

Unlike basic websites that just guess the size, our tool runs a Live Browser Engine. When you move the slider, it actually compresses the image in real-time inside your browser and calculates the exact Output KB.

100% Private: Your images are NEVER uploaded to any server. The compression happens securely on your own device.
